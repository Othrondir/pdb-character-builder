## Decisions Made

- **Lock-forward rather than reproduce-then-fix:** RED spec passed on first run, confirming the UAT-reported behavior was already corrected by Phase 12.2-02 (selectFoundationSummary refactor that moved label readout to `getLabelById`, a validation-independent path). Rather than contrive a regression, shipped the spec as a permanent lock.
- **Direct fixture lookup for labels:** Even though `selectFoundationSummary` currently returns labels correctly, the stepper now resolves labels via `phase03FoundationFixture.races.find(...)` and `.alignments.find(...)` directly. This severs the rail from the composed summary so no future change to `selectFoundationSummary` (e.g. null-gating under invalid state for a different feature) can leak into the rail.
- **Narrow subscriptions + getState() in Atributos branch:** The three top-level narrow selectors keep the hook reactive to every field any branch consumes; the Atributos branch reads `useCharacterFoundationStore.getState()` for the wider helpers without adding a wide subscription. Marked `baseAttributes` as `void baseAttributes` to document the intentional reactive-only use.
