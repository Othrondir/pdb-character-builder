**2. Uneven tab spacing** — flex:1 made all tabs equal width, creating visual imbalance. Fixed with natural width + `justify-content: center`.

**3. Deity step removal** — User identified deity as irrelevant for this tool (server-managed). Removed from the entire origin flow across 9 files.
