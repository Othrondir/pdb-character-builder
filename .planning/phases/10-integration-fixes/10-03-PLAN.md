<objective>
Close the two integration-hygiene gaps flagged by the v1.0 milestone audit for Phase 10:

1. **Shell `validationStatus` cleanup (VALI-01).** A grep over `apps/planner/src` shows only two hits for `validationStatus`, both inside `planner-shell.ts` itself (the field declaration + initial value). No readers. The field is orphan state — delete it.

Foundation setters (`setRace`, `setSubrace`, `setAlignment`) already accept `CanonicalId | null`, so the cleanest fix is to dispatch `null` directly and drop the cast.

Purpose: Eliminate orphan shell state and schema-hostile sentinel values that the audit identified as latent bugs. Both are small, contained, independently verifiable.
Output: Updated `planner-shell.ts`, updated `origin-board.tsx`, two small Vitest suites pinning the fixes.
